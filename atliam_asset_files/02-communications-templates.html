Atlanta IAM Q1 2025 — Escaping Legacy Gravity with Identity

We are pleased to invite you to the Atlanta IAM User Group quarterly event on March 31, 2025. This quarter's session focuses on AI-driven legacy IAM modernization — one of the most pressing challenges facing enterprise identity teams today.

Our featured presentation, "Escaping Legacy Gravity with Identity," will be co-presented by Nishad Sankaranarayanan (Global Director, Cybersecurity, Genuine Parts Company) and Craig Baltes (Aflac), sponsored by Ping Identity.

"Identity modernization isn't just a technology problem — it requires the courage to decommission and subtract, not just accumulate."

Atlanta IAM is a practitioner-first community committed to vendor-neutral, peer-to-peer knowledge sharing. Our events bridge the gap between vendor hype and real-world implementation challenges across enterprise identity programs.

We look forward to seeing you there. For registration and full event details, please visit atlantaiam.com.
